#### Sustainability math (Atlas F baseline)

- **Target output:** ~200 ℓ/week meets “war-coven” demand **plus** 100 ℓ surplus for commerce.    
- **Crimson Reserve** can divert 80–100 ℓ/week from “expired” bags.    
- **Vitae Alchemy** bioreactor (1 000 ℓ fermenter, 28-day cycle) yields ~250 ℓ/month of RedSeal.    
- **Lazarus Club** (8 guests, 0.45 ℓ/night) adds 25 ℓ/week.    

Combined, you exceed 300 ℓ/week—enough to supply neighboring domains while keeping your own stores buffered against siege.

### Net effect on reputation

1. **Reliability:** Elders remember Vienna’s chaos; you answer with plenty.    
2. **Virtuosity:** Seamless blend of thaumaturgy, biotech, and mortal fronts showcases classic Tremere ingenuity.    
3. **Reciprocity:** You do not hoard; you _sell_ and _gift_ at strategic moments, turning vitae into political equity.    

Deliver on any two of these schemes, and the clan’s old guard will whisper that a ninth-generation sorcerer has done what pontifices failed to do: make the Pyramid indispensable again—one liter at a time.
