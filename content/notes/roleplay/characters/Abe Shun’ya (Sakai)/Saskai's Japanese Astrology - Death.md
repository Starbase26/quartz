# 🕯 Esoteric Turning Reading — Becoming Undead  
**Date of Transformation:** Meiji 22, 3rd Month, 12th Day

---

## ⚙️ 1. Sexagenary Pillar: 庚辰 (Kanoe-Tatsu)

### 庚 (Kanoe) — Yang Metal
- Hardness, severance, judgment, ritual violence
- Pure Yang: willpower, destruction, weaponry

### 辰 (Tatsu) — Dragon
- Celestial boundary sign; bridges Earth and Heaven
- Symbol of transformation, fate, and latent power

### Combined Meaning:  
- **Kanoe-Tatsu (庚辰)** = The "Iron Dragon"
- Destruction that awakens, death that births power
- A sign of **violent severance followed by altered rebirth**

> Perfectly suited to a transformation into the undead:
> - **Kanoe** severs life  
> - **Tatsu** bridges death and spiritual power

---

## 📅 2. Historical Context: Meiji 22 (1889)

- Year of the **Meiji Constitution** (promulgated February 11)
- Japan transitioning from spiritual cosmology to **legal-rational statehood**
- Decline of the Onmyōji and collapse of imperial ritual cosmology

> Transformation into undeath at this moment marks a being
> **cut loose from all orders — ancestral, spiritual, and legal**.  
> Born into a **rupture between eras**, belonging to neither.

## 🌘 3. Moon Phase: Waning Gibbous

- Occurs just after the full moon; descent into shadow
- Phase of **release**, **spiritual dispersion**, and **loss of mortal energy**
- Associated with **yūrei**, lingering spirits, and ancestral unrest

> A turning at this time makes for a being **freed from rebirth**,  
> but also **unanchored**, with no clear place in the living or dead world.

## 🧭 4. Elemental Relationship

- **Metal (庚)** is born from **Earth (辰)**
- Power structure: **Destruction fed by foundation**
- Symbolism:
  - Power drawn from **bones, graves, and soil**
  - **Sealed, timeless will** drawn from the underworld or ancestral realm

> Suggests a being reborn as **a blade beneath the mountain**:  
> **Dead to time**, yet rooted in fate, memory, or curse.
