## Subject to Change!

1. **Generation 9 == Power + Plausibility**
	- 9th gen is powerful but not ancient - a perfect tier for someone with ambition, resources, and influence, but still young enough to push for reform
	- Old enough to remember the pyramid, even having served under the old hierarchy, but not so invested to be nostalgic about it.
2. **Post-Chantries == Power vacuum**
	- After the fall of the Vienna Chantry and the 2nd Inquisition’s purges, the Tremere are scattered, suspicious, and bitterly divided
	- Many fledgeling thaumaturges are lost without guidance: rogues are inventing their own paths, and elders hoard what little is left
	- My Character fills a power vacuum: a **visionary, unafraid to rebuild**, but savy enough to avoid the mistakes of the past.
3. **Modernisation vs Tradition == Built-in Conflict**
	- You can define your modernisation in meaningful ways:
		- Networked Chantries - operating  like distributed cells in stead of a top-down dogma
		- Decentralized ritual libraries, digitally encrypted  and spread across hidden servers
		- Recruitment through universities, occult groups, hackerspaces, or online cults
		- Adapting Sorcery to modern needs: information warfare, crypto-rituals, biohacking
