# Divine Rank Hierarchy (1-9)

| Tier  | Rank                              | Description                                                                                                                           | Power level  |
| ----- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| **1** | **Initiate Spark**                | A mortal bearing a nascent divine spark—chosen by fate, prophecy, or divine will. Their power is unstable and largely symbolic.       | 0            |
| **2** | **Demigod**                       | A being of mixed mortal and divine essence, or one partially elevated through heroism or divine favor. Limited but real divine power. | 1-5          |
| **3** | **Godling**                       | A newly awakened divine entity gaining stability. Still dependent on faith or relics, but beginning to form domains and followings.   | 6-10         |
| **4** | **Lesser Deity**                  | A localized or narrowly focused deity. Often worshipped by small cultures, sects, or specific professions.                            | 11-15        |
| **5** | **Intermediate Deity**            | Commands multiple domains and a wide array of followers. Capable of divine intervention and shaping planar regions.                   | 16-20        |
| **6** | **Greater Deity**                 | Influences entire civilizations or continents. Central to major pantheons and myth cycles.                                            | 21-25        |
| **7** | **Prime Deity**                   | One of the great architects of existence. Their influence spans planes and epochs. Capable of reshaping creation itself.              | 32-64        |
| **8** | **Eternal**                       | Beyond time and entropy. Their existence is immutable, often tied to cosmic truths or axioms.                                         | 128-256      |
| **9** | **Orryn** (Transcendent Absolute) | A state beyond divinity—pure will, origin, or law. Not worshipped so much as observed through paradox and principle.                  | 512-infinity |

#### Notes

- **Tiers 1-4** are ideal for PCs, demigod campaigns, and cult conflicts.    
- **Tiers 5-7** support full pantheon building, divine politics, or godwars.    
- **Tiers 8-10** are cosmic-level entities—mystery, apocalypse, and metaphysical revelation.    

---

- **Tier 7 (Prime Deity):** Doubling power every step beyond Tier 6; hence, starting around `2^5 = 32`.   
- **Tier 8 (Eternal):** Reflects `2^7 to 2^8 = 128-256` range, suggesting command over universal constants.    
- **Tier 9 (Orryn):** Starts at `2^9 = 512` and moves toward infinity, symbolizing unknowable or concept-level divinity (e.g., "Time," "Entropy," "Void").

# Detailed Concepts

### Avatar Limit

Represents the number of physical or metaphysical manifestations a deity can simultaneously project into the cosmos. Avatars range from whispering dreams (T2) to fully autonomous planar agents (T6+).

### Domain Breadth

Refers to the thematic and metaphysical scope of a deity's power. Deities of higher tiers not only cover more domains but also govern the rules by which those domains function.

### Artifact Thresholds

Defines how many powerful objects or relics the deity can imbue, maintain, or bind their essence to — scaling from minor icons (blessed items) to world-shaping relics or even reality cores.

## Tier Summaries

### Initiate Spark
- Power: 0  
- Avatars: 0  
- Domain: One proto-domain ("Hope", "Change")  
- Artifacts: Attune 1 minor relic (no creation)

### Demigod
- Power: 1-5  
- Avatars: 1 (semi-corporeal/dream-based)  
- Domain: 1 minor domain (inherited/gifted)  
- Artifacts: 1 minor artifact (needs co-sponsorship)

### Godling
- Power: 6-10  
- Avatars: 1-2  
- Domain: 1 full domain ("Fire", "Hunt")  
- Artifacts: Create 1-2 minor artifacts or empower 1 major

### Lesser Deity
- Power: 11-15  
- Avatars: 2-3  
- Domain: Up to 2 domains (regional)  
- Artifacts: 3-5 minor, 1 major artifact

### Intermediate Deity
- Power: 16-20  
- Avatars: 4-6  
- Domain: 2-3 domains (global/planar)  
- Artifacts: 5-7 major, 1 greater relic

### Greater Deity
- Power: 21-25  
- Avatars: 7-10  
- Domain: 3+ domains (planetary)  
- Artifacts: 10 major, 2-3 greater relics

### Prime Deity
- Power: 32-64  
- Avatars: 10-15  
- Domain: Portfolio (concepts like Justice, Death)  
- Artifacts: Infinite minor, 5-7 greater, 1 artifact plane

### Eternal
- Power: 128-256  
- Avatars: Infinite (self-replicating)  
- Domain: Absolute concept control (Time, Space)  
- Artifacts: Create cosmic artifacts, reforge divine essence

### Orryn
- Power: 512-infinity  
- Avatars: Beyond avatar limits  
- Domain: Archetypal principles (Origin, Void)  
- Artifacts: Create/dissolve planes, birth/absorb gods
